Digital Maha E Seva Kendra primarily provides in-office and digital facilitation services. This Shipping Policy explains how documents or outputs are delivered to customers.

1. Service Delivery Mode

Most services are completed digitally or through government portals. Customers are generally required to visit our office for application submission, biometric processes (if required), and document collection.

2. Office Collection

Completed documents, printouts, acknowledgements, or certificates (where applicable) are normally handed over at our office:

Address: Chandratirth Building, Shri Renuka Temple Back Side, Near Renuka Sports, Yallama Chowk, Ishwarpur, Walwa, Sangli, Maharashtra - 415409

Physical shipping/courier of documents is not a standard service. In exceptional cases, if courier delivery is requested and agreed:

Where possible, acknowledgements, application numbers, or soft copies may be shared via WhatsApp/email as per customer request and service type.

5. Customer Responsibility
