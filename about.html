Who We Are

Digital Maha E Seva Kendra

Digital Maha E Seva Kendra is a trusted government service center located in Ishwarpur, Walwa, Sangli. We provide various Government and Online Services under one roof, assisting citizens with Aadhaar services, PAN Card, Passport, Tahasil Office work, Government Certificates, and many other online services.

Our center is committed to delivering quick, secure, and professional services to every citizen. With years of experience and a dedicated team, we have become the preferred choice for government documentation in the Sangli district.

Tagline: Your One Stop Solution for Government & Online Services

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and efficient government and online services to every citizen in our community. We aim to simplify the process of obtaining government documents and certificates, making it hassle-free for everyone.

We strive to be the most reliable Maha E Seva Kendra by offering transparent services, professional guidance, and timely delivery of all applications and certificates.

Our Vision

To become the leading digital government service center in Maharashtra, empowering citizens with easy access to all government services under one roof.

We envision a future where every citizen can complete their government documentation needs quickly and securely, without visiting multiple offices or facing unnecessary delays.
